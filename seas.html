Save Our Seas

Why Seas Matter?

Our oceans are the blue heart of our planet, regulating our climate, providing food, and generating over half of the oxygen we breathe. From the sunlit surface to the mysterious depths, they teem with life essential to our survival. Yet, this vital life-support system is under siege from pollution, over-exploitation, and a changing climate.

This page explores the urgent threats facing our marine worlds and the critical actions we must take to preserve them.p
