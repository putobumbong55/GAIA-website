GAIA works to protect our world's most vital ecosystems from immediate threats. Your support fuels our guided mission.

Our Work

Protect Our Forests

Safeguarding the world's lungs from deforestation and promoting sustainable management to preserve biodiversity and combat climate change.

Combating plastic pollution, protecting marine life, and restoring coral reefs to ensure healthy oceans for future generations.

Empower Communities

Mobilising grassroots movements and promoting sustainable practices to creating lasting environmental change from the ground up.
