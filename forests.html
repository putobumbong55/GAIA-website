Protect the Forests

Why Forests Matter?

Forests are the vibrant, breathing heart of our planet. They are essential life-support systems, stabilizing our climate, housing incredible biodiversity, and providing resources for billions.Yet, these vital ecosystems are under unprecedented threat.

This page explores the challenges our forests face and the powerful solutions we can support together to ensure they thrive for generations to come.

The Threats Our Forests Face

  • Deforestation & Clear-Cutting

    The permanent removal of trees for agriculture and urban expansion, leading to habitat loss and carbon release.

  • Climate Change Impacts

    Rising temperatures causing more intense wildfires and spreading tree-killing pests.

  • Biodiversity Loss

    Habitat destruction causing species extinction and collapsing delicate ecosystems.
